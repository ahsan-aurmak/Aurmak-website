Case Study · Industrial

FieldSense Maintenance AI.

A distributed maintenance operation needed better planning precision and fewer repeat breakdowns. We introduced AI-assisted maintenance planning with practical workflow controls.

Discuss Similar Programme Back to Case Studies

Project Snapshot

Client Type
Industrial maintenance services group
Region
GCC
Delivery Window
15 weeks
Engagement
AI planning and field mobility integration

The Challenge

Maintenance planning depended on manual judgement, creating uneven task quality and recurring equipment faults. Teams lacked a consistent prioritisation model.

What We Built

We integrated AI-assisted planning recommendations into existing maintenance workflows with controls for supervisor review and field execution.

  • Fault pattern analysis linked to preventive task scheduling.
  • Work-order prioritisation with operational criticality scoring.
  • Mobile-ready field execution views for technicians and supervisors.

Outcomes

  • 29% reduction in repeat faults on monitored assets.
  • 41% improvement in technician utilisation efficiency.
  • 34% faster maintenance planning cycle for weekly schedules.
