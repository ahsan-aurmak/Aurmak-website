SiteFlow PMO Dashboard.

A multi-project construction programme needed dependable visibility across schedule, cost, and procurement signals. We built a PMO dashboard that unified delivery governance across stakeholders.

Case Study Overview

Project Snapshot

Client Type
Property development group
Region

Delivery Window
14 weeks
Engagement
PMO dashboard and workflow automation

The Challenge

Project teams relied on manual updates from different contractors and formats. Leadership reviews were delayed and risk escalation often arrived too late.

What We Built

We implemented a construction control tower that connected planning inputs, procurement checkpoints, and on-site progress into one reporting layer.

  • Automated daily progress ingestion from site teams.
  • Risk heatmap with variance and delayed-milestone flags.
  • Weekly board-ready pack generated from live project data.

Outcomes

  • 31% reduction in manual reporting effort for PMO teams.
  • 22% improvement in schedule adherence on active projects.
  • Single governance workflow across client, consultant, and contractor groups.
