About AURMAK

Global Vision, Local Execution.

AURMAK operates as a dual-track technology company designed to capitalise on market inefficiencies. We utilise a "Global HQ / Local Delivery" model to combine strategic leadership in London and Dubai with a high-performance engineering hub in Lahore. Our approach bypasses the traditional trust barriers of new firms by offering "Day 1 Trust" through our international parentage.

London

Global headquarters focused on governance, commercial direction, and venture portfolio management.

Dubai

Regional delivery and client coordination centre for the Gulf, supporting enterprise implementation and account operations.

Lahore

Engineering and integration centre driving software development, testing, and systems modernising programmes.
