Case Study · Industrial

PlantOS Modern ERP Core.

A manufacturing operation was constrained by ageing ERP modules and disconnected workflows. We delivered a phased modernisation programme without disrupting production continuity.

Discuss Similar Programme Back to Case Studies

Project Snapshot

Client Type
Manufacturing plant network
Region
Pakistan
Delivery Window
20 weeks
Engagement
ERP modernisation and security hardening

The Challenge

Legacy workflows depended on spreadsheet workarounds and unsupported modules. Core approvals lacked traceability and process latency affected operational planning.

What We Built

We re-architected core ERP modules, introduced governance checkpoints, and migrated data in controlled phases aligned with plant operations.

  • Procurement, inventory, and maintenance modules rebuilt for scale.
  • Role-based security model with full audit tracing.
  • Phased migration plan with rollback controls and validation gates.

Outcomes

  • 47% reduction in process latency across critical workflows.
  • 100% audit trail completeness for approvals and exceptions.
  • 26% improvement in planning accuracy for production support functions.
